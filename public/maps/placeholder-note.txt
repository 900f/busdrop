Place your Fortnite map images here:
  public/maps/current.jpg  — Chapter 7 Season 2 map
  public/maps/og.jpg       — OG/Chapter 1 map

Recommended resolution: 1024x1024 or 2048x2048 JPEG.
You can find high-quality map images at:
  https://fortnite.gg/map
  https://progameguides.com/fortnite/fortnite-map/

The MapCanvas component will show a grid fallback if images are missing.
